About the topic

  • Similar as in many countries, population in Croatia is ageing.
  • We use Shiny application to demonstrate some aspects of ageing in Croatia
  • App location: https://dmundjar.shinyapps.io/PopulationDynamicsCroatia
  • Data source: Croatian bureau of statistics (www.dzs.hr)
  • Publication: Population Projections of the Republic of Croatia 2010-2061, (2011)
  • Web: www.dzs.hr/Hrv_Eng/Projections/projekcije_stanovnistva_2010-2061.pdf
